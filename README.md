# Project: Sanity

## About the Repository

This GitHub repository will serve as the central hub for all developers working on the project, providing a single, organized space to access essential information and stay aligned as a team. It will be used to share progress updates, track changes, and communicate ongoing work so that everyone remains informed and coordinated throughout development.

In addition, the repository will house all game-related documentation and resources, including design documents, technical specifications, asset references, and implementation guidelines. By centralizing these materials, the repo will help streamline workflows, reduce miscommunication, and ensure that all team members have the tools and context they need to contribute effectively to the project.

---

## About the Project

Set in 2087 New York, you take the role of an unnamed Private investigator, drawn into a case in the central district of the city because of a string of unexplained disappearances tied to one hotel. Beneath the polished exterior of a once-thriving hotel hides something far more sinister: a cult lurking in the shadows. Across three escalating levels, you will descend deeper into the unknown, confronting its followers and uncovering a truth never meant to be surfaced and the ultimate goal of the Cult.

---

## The vision

An FPS, offense-driven, shield-centric experience where players are rewarded for precise timing, positioning, and mastery of momentum. Set within tightly interwoven environments inspired by cyberpunk and Dishonored, the game challenges players to constantly make tactical decisions, whether to dominate encounters head-on or slow down, observe, and move unseen.

At its core is a focused, small-scale narrative: a cult hidden within a hotel, abducting individuals in an attempt to fuse a human host with an incomprehensibly advanced AI. This story is not told through cutscenes or heavy dialogue, but through the environment itself, uncovered piece by piece as the player investigates deeper.

The visual identity reflects the player’s descent, shifting from grounded and familiar to dark and oppressive, and ultimately into something otherworldly and incomprehensible.

---

## Design Pillars

### The shield at the center of gameplay
The shield at the center of gameplay: The shield is the core of the player’s experience, serving as both weapon and defense. Every system is built around mastering its mechanics, from precise throws and rebounds to timing, positioning, and momentum.

### Playing as an investigator
Playing as an investigator: The player is not just a fighter, but an investigator. Progression is driven by exploration, observation, and uncovering hidden truths within the environment, rather than direct exposition.

### Lovecraftian horror undertones in the Cyberpunk-like future
Lovecraftian horror undertones in the Cyberpunk-like future: A cyberpunk-inspired world layered with Lovecraftian horror. Advanced technology and incomprehensible forces intertwine, creating a setting where the line between machine, mind, and something far beyond begins to collapse.
